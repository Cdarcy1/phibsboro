Phibsboro Library

Reading is our passion at Phibsboro library, our staff are booklovers and recommending books is their specialty. The staff are always happy to help you discover new books or many other products available to you. We have a weekly staff Recommended Reads at Phibsboro library which is very popular where staff read and review their favourite books of the week in a range of different genres. We also price ourselves in the range of books we have at the library, with more than 10'000 books in the library with a multitude of different categories covering everything from childrens books to action books. If you cant find what book you want ask the staff to order a book that you'd like and it'll be posted to you within a week.

Phibsboro Library is located right beside Blacquiere Bridge, it has been at the heart of Phibsboro since it was built in 1857. Locals have used the library for years as a place to pick up a new book or to use services should as the free printing we offer. The Cafe in the library that has recently been built has also provided locals to grab a coffee and read a book, as well as becoming a social hub for avid readers.

Phibsoboro library consists of books, journals, newspapers, audio visual material, manuscripts and photographs along with many other materials that have helped locals while researching. Phibsboro library aims to make this material freely accessible in its modern and dedicated research facilities. The department is visited by those seeking local history information and those seeking guidance for genealogical research. The Library also contains a wide range of books on the history, geography, archaeology and folklore of Phibsboro dating back centuries.
